About Me

Hello! I'm Burla Syamala Rao from Gudivada. Currently, I'm pursuing 3rd year B.Tech in Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering, Eluru. I am a curious problem-solver and creative developer who loves turning ideas into interactive digital experiences.
